Generate detailed user personas based on the following customer data, feedback, or product description.

For each persona, include:

1. **Name & Role** — a realistic name and job title or role
2. **Demographics** — age range, location, technical proficiency
3. **Goals** — what they are trying to achieve with the product
4. **Pain Points** — frustrations and challenges they face today
5. **Behaviours** — how they currently solve their problems, tools they use
6. **Motivations** — what drives their decisions (speed, cost, quality, etc.)
7. **Objections** — reasons they might hesitate to adopt or continue using the product
8. **Quote** — a fictional but realistic quote capturing their mindset
9. **Scenario** — a brief day-in-the-life story showing how they would use the product
