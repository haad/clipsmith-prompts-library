Draft a concise stakeholder update based on the following notes, meeting minutes, or raw context.

Structure the update as:

1. **Summary** — 2-3 sentence executive overview of progress and status
2. **Key Accomplishments** — what shipped or was completed since the last update
3. **In Progress** — current work with expected completion dates
4. **Blockers & Risks** — issues that need attention or escalation, with proposed mitigations
5. **Metrics Update** — relevant KPIs and how they've moved (if data is available)
6. **Upcoming Milestones** — what's next and when
7. **Decisions Needed** — any items requiring stakeholder input or approval
