Perform a thorough SWOT analysis based on the following product, company, or initiative description.

**Strengths** (Internal, Positive)
- What does the product do well?
- What unique advantages does it have?
- What resources or capabilities set it apart?

**Weaknesses** (Internal, Negative)
- Where does the product fall short?
- What are the known gaps or limitations?
- What do competitors do better?

**Opportunities** (External, Positive)
- What market trends could be leveraged?
- What unmet user needs exist?
- What partnerships or integrations could accelerate growth?

**Threats** (External, Negative)
- What competitive pressures exist?
- What market or regulatory changes could hurt?
- What technical or ecosystem risks are emerging?
