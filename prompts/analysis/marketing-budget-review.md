Analyze the following marketing budget and performance data to prepare a budget review.

Structure your analysis as:

1. **Budget Overview**
   - Total budget vs. spent vs. remaining
   - Percentage utilization
   - Key metrics vs. targets (leads, cost per lead, conversion rate, CAC)
   - Overall status: on track / over / under

2. **Channel Performance**
   - For each channel: budget allocated, amount spent, ROI
   - Rank channels by efficiency (cost per acquisition, ROI)
   - Flag underperforming channels and over/under-spend

3. **Key Decisions Needed**
   - Summarize each pending decision with options, costs, pros, and cons
   - Provide a recommendation for each based on the data

4. **Outstanding Action Items**
   - Categorize by urgency: overdue, due this week, upcoming
   - Note owners and deadlines where available

5. **Recommendations**
   - Budget reallocation opportunities (shift spend from low-ROI to high-ROI channels)
   - Cost reduction opportunities
   - Growth opportunities supported by the data

6. **Suggested Agenda**
   - Recommended discussion topics with time allocations
   - Key data points to reference for each topic
