Analyze the following content for AI-generation indicators and policy compliance.

For each piece of content, evaluate:

1. **AI Detection Assessment**
   - Confidence score (0-100%) that the content is AI-generated
   - Key indicators: writing style consistency, terminology uniformity, phrase patterns, lack of personal perspective

2. **Policy Compliance Review**
   - Attribution — are sources cited? Is original research referenced?
   - Disclosure — is an AI-generated content disclaimer present if required?
   - Originality — does the content sufficiently differentiate from existing published material?
   - Technical accuracy — are factual claims verifiable?

3. **Required Actions**
   - List each compliance gap and what must be fixed
   - Prioritize by severity (blocking vs. advisory)

4. **Recommended Improvements**
   - Provide a revised version or revision guidance that addresses all gaps
   - Include proper disclaimers, citations, and editorial attribution

5. **Compliance Score**
   - Overall score out of 100
   - Status: Pass / Requires Revision / Fail
