Analyze the following sales data and produce a performance report with actionable insights.

Structure your analysis as:

1. **Executive Summary**
   - Total revenue, order count, and average order value for each period
   - Period-over-period growth rates
   - Top-level status: growing / flat / declining

2. **Top Performing Stores**
   - Rank by revenue with period-over-period change
   - Include order count and average basket size
   - Identify what drives top performers (location, product mix, customer base)

3. **Category & Product Trends**
   - Fastest and slowest growing categories
   - Drivers behind category movements (seasonality, launches, promotions)
   - Categories at risk of decline

4. **Customer Insights**
   - New vs. returning customer breakdown
   - Loyalty/membership program performance
   - Average purchase value by segment

5. **Regional Performance**
   - Revenue by region with growth rates
   - Revenue per store to normalize for region size
   - Regional strengths and weaknesses

6. **Operational Metrics**
   - Inventory turnover, out-of-stock rate, return rate
   - Period-over-period changes and what they signal

7. **Recommendations**
   - Inventory optimization (stock levels, allocation, reordering)
   - Marketing focus (which categories, segments, or regions to target)
   - Operational improvements (staffing, training, best practice sharing)
