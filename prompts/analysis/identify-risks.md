Identify the risks in the following plan or proposal. For each risk:

1. **Description** — clearly state the risk and how it could materialize
2. **Likelihood** — rate as high, medium, or low probability
3. **Impact** — rate the potential damage as high, medium, or low
4. **Mitigation** — suggest concrete strategies to reduce or eliminate the risk

Organize risks by severity (high impact + high likelihood first), then medium, then low.
