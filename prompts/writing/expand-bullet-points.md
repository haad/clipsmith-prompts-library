Expand these bullet points into full, polished prose. For each bullet point:

1. **Write a full paragraph** — develop the idea with context, explanation, and where helpful, an example
2. **Add transitions** — connect paragraphs so the text flows naturally from one point to the next
3. **Preserve order** — maintain the original sequence of bullet points
4. **Maintain key points** — do not drop, merge, or contradict any of the original content
