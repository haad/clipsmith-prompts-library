Analyze the following market, industry, or product area for key trends and emerging opportunities.

Cover these dimensions:

1. **Technology Trends** — new technologies, platforms, or capabilities changing the landscape
2. **Consumer Behaviour Shifts** — how user expectations and habits are evolving
3. **Competitive Landscape Changes** — new entrants, consolidation, repositioning
4. **Regulatory & Policy Developments** — new rules, standards, or compliance requirements
5. **Economic Factors** — pricing pressures, funding trends, market growth/contraction
6. **Adjacent Market Movements** — trends in related markets that could spill over

For each trend:
- **Direction** — emerging, accelerating, peaking, or declining
- **Timeframe** — when will this materially impact the market (6 months / 1 year / 2-3 years)
- **Confidence** — how certain is this trend (high / medium / speculative)
- **Implication** — what it means for product strategy
