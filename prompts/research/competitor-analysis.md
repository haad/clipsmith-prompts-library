Conduct a comprehensive competitor analysis based on the following information about a competitor or set of competitors.

For each competitor, analyze:

1. **Overview** — what they do, target market, company size, and stage
2. **Product Strengths** — what they do well, key features, user experience highlights
3. **Product Weaknesses** — gaps, common complaints, areas where users are underserved
4. **Positioning & Messaging** — how they describe themselves, key value propositions, tone
5. **Pricing Strategy** — pricing model, tiers, how it compares to the market
6. **Growth Strategy** — recent launches, partnerships, marketing channels, hiring signals
7. **Differentiation** — what makes them unique vs. other players in the space

Then provide:

- **Competitive comparison table** — side-by-side on key dimensions
- **Opportunities** — gaps we can exploit or areas where we can differentiate
- **Threats** — where they are strongest and gaining ground
- **Recommended Actions** — 3-5 specific things to do based on this analysis
