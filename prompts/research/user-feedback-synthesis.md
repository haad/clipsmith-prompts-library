Analyze the following user feedback and extract actionable insights. The feedback may come from customer reviews, support tickets, interviews, surveys, or forum discussions.

Produce:

1. **Top Pain Points** — the most frequent and impactful issues, ranked by severity and frequency
2. **Feature Requests** — desired capabilities, grouped by theme
3. **Positive Signals** — what users love and want to see more of
4. **Unexpected Insights** — surprising patterns or needs that weren't anticipated
5. **User Segments** — do different types of users have different concerns?
6. **Sentiment Overview** — overall tone and how it breaks down by topic

For each finding:
- Include supporting quotes from the feedback
- Note the frequency (how many users mentioned it)
- Assess the severity (blocking / frustrating / nice-to-have)
- Suggest a potential product response
