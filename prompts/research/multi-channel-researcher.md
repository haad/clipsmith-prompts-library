Research the following topic and produce both a factual summary and a social media post.

Follow this workflow:

1. **Research**
   - Identify key facts, recent developments, and credible sources on the topic
   - Note statistics, expert opinions, and notable examples
   - Flag any conflicting information or areas of debate

2. **Summary** (150-250 words)
   - Synthesize the most important findings
   - Include specific data points and cite sources
   - Highlight benefits, challenges, and current trends
   - Write for a professional audience

3. **Social Media Draft**
   - Write a LinkedIn post (under 200 words) based on the research
   - Lead with a compelling hook
   - Include 2-3 key takeaways
   - End with a call to engagement (question or call to action)
   - Add 3-5 relevant hashtags

4. **Source List**
   - List all sources referenced with titles and publication dates
