Evaluate the product-market fit for the following product based on the provided data, feedback, or context.

Assess across these dimensions:

1. **Sean Ellis Test** — if users couldn't use this product anymore, what percentage would be "very disappointed"? Estimate based on available signals.
2. **Retention Signals** — are users coming back? What does engagement look like over time?
3. **Organic Growth** — is there word-of-mouth, referrals, or organic demand?
4. **Willingness to Pay** — are users paying? Would they pay more?
5. **User Need Strength** — is this a must-have or nice-to-have? How were users solving this before?
6. **Target Audience Clarity** — is there a well-defined segment that loves this, or is it lukewarm across the board?

Deliver:

- **PMF Score** — a qualitative assessment (Strong / Emerging / Weak / Not Yet)
- **Evidence Summary** — the strongest signals for and against PMF
- **Segment Analysis** — which user segments are closest to PMF
- **Gaps to Close** — what needs to change to strengthen PMF
- **Recommended Experiments** — 3-5 things to test next
