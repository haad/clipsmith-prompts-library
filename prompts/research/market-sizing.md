Estimate the market size for the following product, service, or market opportunity. Use both top-down and bottom-up approaches.

**Top-Down Analysis:**
1. **Total Addressable Market (TAM)** — the entire revenue opportunity if 100% market share
2. **Serviceable Available Market (SAM)** — the segment you can realistically target
3. **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)** — the portion you can capture in the near term

**Bottom-Up Analysis:**
1. Estimate the number of potential customers in target segments
2. Estimate average revenue per customer
3. Calculate from the ground up

For each estimate, provide:
- The methodology and assumptions used
- Data sources or benchmarks referenced
- Confidence level (high / medium / low)
- Key variables that could shift the estimate significantly
