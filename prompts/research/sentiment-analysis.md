Analyze the following data for shifts in consumer sentiment, emerging preferences, and potential dissatisfaction trends.

Examine:

1. **Overall Sentiment** — positive, negative, neutral breakdown with confidence
2. **Trending Topics** — themes gaining traction (positive or negative)
3. **Sentiment by Category** — break down sentiment by product area, feature, or topic
4. **Emerging Preferences** — new behaviours or expectations appearing in the data
5. **Risk Signals** — early indicators of dissatisfaction that could grow
6. **Competitive Mentions** — how competitors are being discussed in context

For each insight:
- Provide specific evidence from the source data
- Note whether the trend is new, growing, stable, or declining
- Assess potential business impact (high / medium / low)
